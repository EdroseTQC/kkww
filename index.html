About Me

About me

Director of Kings Kingdom World Wide. I am

Born and raised in Ghana. I am a Politician by profession, a Human Rights and Freedom Activist, and a Motivational Speaker. I love to get to the ground level to find out the needs of people who are under-privileged in my community, that is, Ada, in order to provide them assistance in the most appropriate and appreciative manner. I love to take up leadership roles in my community in order to be in the best position to assist the youth, most especially, girls and women. Over the years, I have successfully undertaken several developmental events and projects in several areas in my community and gained the recognition of the minister of gender, youth and sports. I represent one thing, which is to be a burning and a shining light to the youth in my generation, especially to girls and women in my community and Ghana at large.

Mission

Female Empowerment

Roselyn and her team empower girls and woman in Ghana, especially in Ada, to stand up to take up leadership roles in their community through education and training at workshops.

Provision Of Support

Roselyn and her team are very addent when it comes to providing financial and material support to deprived communities in Ghana, especially in Ada.

Organization Of Communal And Developmental Projects

Roselyn and her team mobilize the youth in communities to undertake communal and developmental activities that includes but not limited to distilling gutters, weeding busy areas, digging drainage systems and organising cultural and quiz competitions for the youth.
